## Don't buy a pig in a poke

Kirby's license model is based on a single idea: you should be happy with Kirby first, before you **(link: buy text: buy a license.)**

Try Kirby on your **local machine** or on a **private test server**. Once you are sure that Kirby fits your project, **(link: buy text: get your license key)** and publish your site.
