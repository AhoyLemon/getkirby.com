Text: Columns made with Kirbytext. Read about extending Kirbytext at <http://getkirby.com/blog/kirbytext>.

**Code at GitHub:**

<https://github.com/NECKRHINOS/kirbytext-columns/blob/master/kirbytext.extended.php>

**Note:**

The grid used relies on the implementation of **Bootstrap 3**.

**Usage:**

<https://github.com/NECKRHINOS/kirbytext-columns#usage>

Two columns:

```
(twocol: start)
Left column
(twocol: break)
Right column
(twocol: end)
```

Three columns:

```
(threecol: start)
Left column
(threecol: break)
Middle column
(threecol: break)
Right column
(threecol: end)
```

Etc.

**Formatting:**

The content of the columns can be formatted with Kirbytext / Markdown, of course.
