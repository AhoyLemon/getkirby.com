Note that if you want to restrict the type of templates users can create at the top level, you can configure site.php as follows:

    pages: 
        template:
            - my_template_1
            - my_template_2
            - my_template_3

see http://getkirby.com/docs/panel/blueprints/page-settings#templates-for-subpages
